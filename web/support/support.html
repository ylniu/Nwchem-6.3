Reporting Problems with NWChem

The nwchem-users email alias is the preferred method of support for NWChem. Please subscribe to the alias and post your questions there.

NWChem User's Mailing List

Subscribe

To be added to the NWChem user's mailing list, please send an e-mail to majordomo@emsl.pnl.gov with the following line in the BODY of the message:

subscribe nwchem-users@emsl.pnl.gov your-email-address

Don't forget to substitute your email address for your-email-address! Also, the ARCHIVES of the messages on the NWChem User's mailing list is now available.

Unsubscribe

To be removed from the NWChem user's mailing list, please send an e-mail to majordomo@emsl.pnl.gov with the following line in the BODY of the message:

unsubscribe nwchem-users@emsl.pnl.gov your-email-address

Global Arrays Support

If you have problems with GA, please checkout the Global Arrays Support Page. If your problem is not resolved there, please send an e-mail to Global Arrays Support with a full description of your problem.
